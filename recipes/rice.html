Rice Recipe

Rice is a versatile and nutritious food, perfect as a base for many meals. Here's how to prepare simple boiled rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water.
  2. In a saucepan, bring the water to a boil.
  3. Add the rice (and salt if using), then stir once.
  4. Cover the pan, reduce heat to low, and let it simmer for 15â€“20 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat and let the rice sit (covered) for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff with a fork and serve.
