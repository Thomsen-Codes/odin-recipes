Sukuma Wiki Recipe

This is a simple recipe on how to cook delicious sukuma wiki (collard greens) the Kenyan way.

Ingredients

  • Sukuma Wiki (collard greens)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash and finely chop the sukuma wiki.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the onions until golden.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add sukuma wiki and stir well.

  6. Add salt to taste and serve hot.
